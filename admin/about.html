About Us

THE PROBLEM

Colliers International currently faces a problem with sharing information across their organization. Their information silos prevent them from capitalizing on opportunities that could be made simply by connecting the dots across databases.

THE SOLUTION

Bontology (business onotology) is a semantic information retrevial and relationship system that allows Colliers to leverage a custom ontology based on vocabulary currently used by their Realtors to help solve a major problem in the ETL (extract, transform, & load) process: term definition ambiguity between analysts and data engineers.

THE RESULT

Bontology provides Colliers a single source of unambiguous, company-specific terms and concepts that will then be used to drive data extraction. This reduces the number of analysts required to perform data cleaning and extraction and allows Colliers to perform more sophisticated data analytics.
